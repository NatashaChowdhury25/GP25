Difficulties People Face

People face a lot of Difficulties when they travel to a different place.Young Peolpe travel for good education , good jobs etc...In this WebPage, you get to see the Difficulties and solutions for problems.

Staying Safe


Difficulties

Many young people are sometimes at the risk of being physically abused and they might not know all the places that could be dangerous.


Solution

Young People should follow rules and should try to avoid unknown, deserted places and should also try to not go out during night time.


Health


Difficulties

As currencies differ from the currency the person might use...so if a person get injured and get a treatment,so the person has to pay a lot of money and the hospitial might not take the currency the person uses.


Solution

The person could do money exchange.


Education


Difficulties

The medium of education might be different.For example european countries will teach in a different medium than asian countries.


Solution

Young People could try and get along with the different teaching method or the could find a school which uses the mathod of teaching they know.


Language


Difficulties

The language might be different so young people might have a hard time communicating.


Solution

Young People could try learning a local language to understand what perople are saying.


Transport


Difficulties

Language of the transport boards may not be in english all the time ,So the person might miss the transport because he/she could not understand what the board said.


Solution

Young People could try learning the language to understand what the board says and the board could also say what it is written in english.


Eating & Drinking



Difficulties

Food and Drinks may give allergies because you might not know what it might contain ,the food may not suit your taste


Solution

Try to cook your own food and also try to stay health by eating vegetables and fruits which are cleaned.
